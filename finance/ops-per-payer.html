About

Operations per Payer Finance Report

The Operations per Payer Finance Report contains the count of claims and transactions handled by Nextcare Claims Management LLC, segragated per Insurer and per Business Unit. Numbers are reported as cumulative total, from January 1st until the last day of the reporting month.



Report Design

Report Design and Components

As of November 2021, the report output is in Microsoft Excel format and has five worksheets, namely:

  • Manual Pre-Certifications
  • Automated Pre-Certifications
  • Direct Billing Claims
  • Reimbursement Claims
  • Care Center Inbound Calls
Two more worksheets are added in May 2022, and the revision was applied to 2021 reports. These are:
  • After Sales Endorsements
  • MCC Retrospective Medical Review

The report consists of columns such as Payer Name, Payer Country, Payer Key and ID, and the corresponding figures per worksheet.

Care Center

Calculation Logic: Care Center Answered Calls

The count of answered calls per payer is calculated using percentage estimation of the total answered calls and total inquiries received per payer.

The method of estimation is used since not all transactions in TATSH Back Office Inquiry Tracking Search data include the payer name, thus the total count of unidentified transactions are spread among payers.

Sample Calculation

Payer Name Inquiries Received per Payer,
from TATSH Back Office
Inquiry Tracking Search data,
with Payer Identifier 		Total Inquiries Received,
from TATSH BO ITS data,
with Payer Identifier 		Percentage of Total Total Answered Calls Final Answered Calls per Payer
