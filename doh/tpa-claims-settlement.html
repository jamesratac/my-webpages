Overview

TPA Claims Settlement

The Department of Health's TPA Claims Settlement report consist of the count and amount of claims processed, ready for settlement, and settled by insurers through Nextcare. The report is required by the Health Regulator of Abu Dhabi to ensure that insurance companies are paying the healthcare providers for medical services rendered. It also includes account receivable and accounts payable aging as per the books of accounts.


Source Data

Source Data

The main source of data is QlikSense BI, from the Nextcare Claims Dashboard.


Scope

Report Scope

The date scope of the report are Direct Billing claims processed within the previous month, based on PO Validation Date.

Claims included are those under the HAAD Licensing Authority.


Submission Deadline

Deadline of Submission

Department of Health requires the report to be uploaded to the DOH Portal seven working days every after last day of the month.

Example: If the month ends on Monday, and UAE working days is Mondays through Fridays, and then the seventh day after the last working month will be Wednesday the following week.


Reminders

Important Notes

  • Make sure not to lock out the account by entering incorrect username and/or password. The credentials are linked to other Nextcare system, such as Greenrain.
  • This is a regulatory requirement so make sure not to miss. Nextcare Compliance receives an email notification from Department of Health once this report has not been submitted after the deadline.


Steps

Reporting Procedure

  1. Download and save the data
    • FROM: Qlik BI > Nextcare Claims Dashboard > Bookmarks = DOH TPA Claims Settlement.
    • TO: \\ncu-tqa-019\DATA\james\__recurring-reports\haad-data-upload\HAAD UPLOAD YEAR 2022
      Note: Create a new folder for the month, or if it's a new year, go one folder up (parent folder) and create a new HAAD UPLOAD YEAR 20XX
  2. Filter the Provider PO Validation Month-Year. This is the only filter that you are required to change.
    The default criteria and filters used are:
    • Licensing Authority = HAAD
    • ASOAP Type = Direct
    • Batch Reception Year <> Null
    • PO Delivery Date <> Null
  3. Go to Department of Health's homepage.
  4. Click Login to DOH
  5. Login using Nextcare credentials
    • Username = Nextcare
    • Password = to be provided by Helen Kay
  6. After logging in, go to: e-Insurance > TPA Forms
    • If in case Finance team already uploaded their data, you will receive an error message = "Data already exists for this month!!!". If you encounter this error, go to (A); else, go to (B).
      • A) Go to e-Insurance > TPA Forms, and then select the latest report saved as draft.
      • B) Add the payers one-by-one by selecting the name of the payer in the dropdown menu and then clicking 'Add Insurer'.
  7. For each payer, click the gear icon to add the figures in the form.
  8. Once data is entered for a payer, click the upload icon to to saved the data.
  9. Once all data have been added, validate the figures by making sure that the totals from Qlik Sense and in the DOH Portal that you have just added are matching.
  10. Once confirmed that the totals matched, go to the bottom of the page and click Submit.
    Note: Never submit the report without data in Table 3 to 7, which are being completed by Finance department.
