Overview

MetLife NIGO Production Dashboard

MetLife's NIGO (Not In Good Order) Production Dashboard shows the count and current status of newly acquired and renewed policies of MetLife, respective of its turnaround time.


Source Data

Source Data

The data used to prepare the dashboard are shared by NIGO team. As of this writing (November 2022), reports are shared by Mariyam Kanorewala.


Reporting Procedures

Reporting Steps

Below are the steps on how to prepare the dashboard:

  1. Open three files:
    • NIGO Dashboard, received from the NIGO team
    • NIGOWorkingFile Excel file, current version is 4.7, saved in the drive
    • NIGO Production Dashboard PPT file, saved in the drive
  2. NIGO Dasboard > New Biz tab: Copy the data in the table, excluding the headers.
  3. NIGOWorkingFile > New Biz tab: Paste the data as values starting Column K: TICKET NO.
  4. NIGO Dasboard > Renewals tab: Copy the data in the table, excluding the headers.
  5. NIGOWorkingFile > Renewals tab: Paste the data as values starting Column K: TICKET NO.
  6. Make sure the formulas in the NIGOWorkingFile New Biz and Renewals tabs are reading all the entries. If not, drag down the formula until the very last transactions.
  7. NIGOWorkingFile > PPT tab: Copy the four tables, and paste to the corresponding tables of the NIGO Production Dashboard PPT file.
