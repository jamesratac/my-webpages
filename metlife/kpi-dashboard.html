Overview

MetLife KPI Dashboard Overview

MetLife KPI Dashboard is a workbook that contains month-to-date status of MetLife claims. It includes information such as count and amount of claims, percentage of rejection, claims' turnaround time, and claims' age. It also contains information about the reporting month's Top X by count and amount, such as top policies, top diagnoses, top healthcare providers, and the list of In-Patient claims that is more than AED 30k.


Source Data

Source Data

Data in the dashboard are extracted from TATSH Web using the following criteria:

  • Service Provider: NEXTCARE Claims Management LLC
  • From Claim Date: Last year's first date of the year
  • To Claim Date: Current year's last date of the year
  • Include BC Claims: Yes
  • Declined: No
  • Not Used Claims: No
  • Payer: MetLife
  • UWYear: 0


Report Design

Report Design and Components

MetLife KPI Dashboard is made up of four worksheets: ADMISSION, RECEIVED, UTILIZATION, and AGEING. Below are the information you can view from each worksheet:

  • ADMISSION worksheet: Contains the total number of claims grouped by Claims Admission Date, and drilled down by (1) ASOAP Type: Direct Billing or Reimbursement; and (2) Family of Cause: In-Patient or Out-Patient. These two classifications are segragated by the claims status: Authorized, Initial, Processed, and Settled.
  • RECEIVED worksheet: Contains the total number of claims grouped by Claims Reception Date. This sheet also has two additional tables compared to Admission worksheet - the Claims Turnaround Time for: (a) Direct Billing and (b) Reimbursement Claims.
  • UTILIZATION worksheet: Shows the previous completed month's claims utilization. It features policies with highest to lowest count of claims for last month, the Top 10 Diagnoses and the Top 10 Healthcare Providers by claim count. You will also find here a list (if applicable) of claims amounting and AED 30,0000 and above.
  • AGEING worksheet: Displays information about claims cycle based on the date of service / admission and reception of claims. Below are the tables you can find in this tab:
    • Based on Claim's Admission Date
      • Incurred to Reported: Claim's age from admission date to reception date.
      • Incurred to Paid: Claim's age from admission date to settlement date.
      • Incurred to PO: Claim's age from admission date to payment order validation date.
    • Based on claim's reception Date
      • Received to PO: Claim's age from reception date to payment order validation date.
      • Received to Paid: Claim's age from reception date to settlement date.
